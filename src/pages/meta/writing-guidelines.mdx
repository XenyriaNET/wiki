# Writing Guidelines

## Writing Style

Consistency in writing style is important to ensure that all wiki pages have a uniform look and feel. Here are some guidelines to follow:

- Use clear and concise language.
- Write in third person point of view.
- Avoid using slang or jargon that may not be familiar to all readers.
- Use active voice instead of passive voice.
- Use proper grammar and spelling.
- Break up long paragraphs into shorter ones for easier reading.
- Use headings and subheadings to organize content.
- Use bullet points and numbered lists to break up information.
- Use italics or bold text for emphasis, but use them sparingly.
- Avoid using all caps, excessive exclamation points, or other formatting that may come across as unprofessional.

By following these guidelines, you can help ensure that the wiki is easy to read and understand for all players.

## Tone and Voice

The tone and voice of the wiki should be informative, helpful, and welcoming. Here are some guidelines to follow:
- Use a friendly and approachable tone.
- Avoid using a condescending or patronizing tone.
- Use positive language to encourage players to participate and engage with the community.
- Avoid using negative language or criticizing players.
- Use humor sparingly and appropriately.
- Be respectful and inclusive of all players, regardless of their background or experience level.
- Avoid using language that could be considered offensive or discriminatory.
- Use examples and anecdotes to help illustrate points and make the content more relatable.
- Use a consistent tone and voice throughout the wiki.
